- How to drink water correctly Click to see full post

- Step 1: perform "optical patdown" to confirm that the substance you are holding is indeed water.

 

- Step 2: double check your water to make sure there is no ice. For a true water drinker the addition of ice is blasphemous. [You will do well to remember that]

 

- Step 3: once it is confirmed you are holding a clean glass of room temperature water it is ok for you to begin the water drinking process. Lift the glass to your mouth and get ready to get quenched.

 

- Step 4: while pouring water into your mouth dont forget to take breaks to breath every now and then. At least for the next 10 years there's plenty of water to go around.

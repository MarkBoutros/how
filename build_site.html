- How to Build a Website

- Step 1: Come up with your site's "Big Idea". Support your general concept by identifying user types through personas and stories.

 

- Step 2: Organize your site's content. Outline how each section works alone and together. Sketches and rough approximations welcome.

 

- Step 3: Think about your site's typography. Does the type suit your site's purpose?

 

- Step 4: Consider your site's composition, does the layout retain it's appeal for multiple sizes of devices?

 

- Step 5: Leverage other design principles to upgrade your site. Consider Dominance, Similarity, Rhythm, Texture, Direction, and Contrast.

 

- Step 6: Decide if your site would benefit from the inclusion of media such as images or videos.

 

- Step 7: Test your site. Take a look from the visitor's perspective to see if there's anything you could improve.

 

- Step 8: Congratulations, you've created your very own site!

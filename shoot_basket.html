- How to shoot a basketball

- Step 1: Balance the ball on the palm of your dominant hand

 

- Step 2: Place your non-dominant hand on the side of the ball

 

- Step 3: place your dominant elbow under the ball

 

- Step 4: look at the rim of the basket

 

- Step 5: shoot the ball and hold your release

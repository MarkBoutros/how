- How to Brush your Teeth

- Step 1: inquire the follow items: a tooth brush, running water, and toothpaste

 

- Step 2: build the tooth brush from the opposite side of the brush

 

- Step 3: make sure the brush part of the toothbrush is facing your teeth

 

- Step 4: wet the brush part of the tooth brush

 

- Step 5: add toothpaste to the brush part of the tooth brush,

 

- Step 6: bring the brush part with the toothpaste on it into your mouth

 

- Step 7: in a circular motion brush your teeth using the brush part of the toothbrush

 

- Step 8: repeat this step 20 times to both the upper and lower teeth

 

- Step 9: remove toothbrush from mouth and run the brush under water

 

- Step 10: add water to your mouth and repeat untill you feel as though all the toothpaste is gone from your mouth

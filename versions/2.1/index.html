How to Send an E-Mail

Step 1: Create an account on a site. I recommend google mail, or Gmail.

 

Step 2: Click the ‘compose’ button in the top right. A ‘new message’ window should pop up.

 

Step 3: Enter the recipient’s email in the ‘to’ text box.

 

Step 4: Write out your email. You can attach images via the paper clip button on the bottom.

 

Step 5: Click send. Congratulations! You have sent an e-mail! Life is meaningless!

How to Walk

Step 1: Stand up on your two legs (they're the ones at the bottom of your body)

 

Step 2: Flex your right quadricep, it's the front of your thigh (that's the top meaty third of your leg)

 

Step 3: Straighten your right leg out

 

Step 4: Lean forward and brace with your right leg

 

Step 5: Catch yourself (do not die!)

 

Step 6: Repeat steps 1-5 for your left leg

 

Step 7: Repeat steps 1-6 until you reach your destination

How to Open a Jar

Step 1: Pickup the Jar.

 

Step 2: Slap the bottom of the jar while holding it upside down.

 

Step 3: Firmly grasp the lid and twist to the left until the lid comes off.
